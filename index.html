Meet Leo

Leo is a domestic shorthair cat. He has a grey coat with dark stripes and his eye color is green. I did rescue him from the streets when he was just a few weeks old, but it is believed that he was born sometime in August 2018.

Favorite Activities

Leo enjoys playing with his toys, sleeping, and spending time outdoors. He especially loves snuggling in bed with me. But his number one favorite activity is eating.

Daily Routine

Leo's daily routine involves waking up the same time I do at 5am and then waits for his automatic feeder to go off shortly after. While I am at work he does a variety of activities such as playing, lounging, staring out of the window and waiting for his second feed timer to go off. Once I get home he immediately gets a serving of wet food while I clean out his litter box. After that he likes to bathe himself and wait for me to give him attention. Once I give him attention, he goes on to do his own things. Finally while I get ready for bed he waits for his third feed timer to go off. After we get in bed and cuddle to sleep.
